# About NSFW Shield

*   🛡️ **Smart AI Protection:** We don't just block a list of websites. Our AI-powered Screen Monitor analyzes content in real-time across all your apps, including social media and browsers, automatically blurring explicit content before you see it.
*   🔒 **Unbeatable Security:** With **Uninstall Protection** and **Admin PIN Locks**, the app cannot be easily bypassed or removed during a moment of weakness.
*   🤝 **Social Accountability:** Connect with an Accountability Partner. Our system sends periodic metadata-only reports to a trusted friend, keeping you honest while keeping your specific browsing history private.
*   ⏳ **Delay-to-Disable:** Our unique security timer requires a 24-hour waiting period before protection can be turned off, helping you overcome impulsive decisions.
*   🚀 **Local & Fast:** Because all filtering and AI analysis happen on your phone, there are no slow proxy servers or "cloud" delays. Your privacy is guaranteed by design.
